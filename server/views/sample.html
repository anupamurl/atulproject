Guidelines

Since the ffirst week is observation week, the food pattern will be similar what was already followed with inclusion of fruits, vegetables and nuts. Regular updates on the whats app group is important. Appreciate your understanding and time. Here are a few guidelines to keep in mind while following the plan:


  • Daily inclusion of 2.5-3 liters of water, 45-60 minutes of exercise and 7-8 hours of sleep is really important.

  • Include fruits as pre workout snacks and direcly have breakfast post workout.

  • For poha and upma you can include a cup of curd for protein. For porridge milk can be included. Since besan and moong dal are already are a protein source, you can have it with chutney.

  • Always opt for natural sources of protein and avoid commercial products.

  • Avoid repeating foods from same food group and do not overdo one single food in a day. Like for example, if you include eggs in the morning, try to avoid it with lunch.

  • If there is constipation, you can include soaked raisins along with warm water in the morning. Raisins need to be soaked overnight and you can consume upon waking. You can discard soaked water.

  • Everything mentioned on the plan are for overall nourishment, so make sure not to skip especialy fruits, vegetables and nuts. Eat seasonal and bring in a lot of variety for foods for diversity in nutrients.

  • For oats porridge , you can use 3tbsp oats, cook it in water and then add milk.

  • Add 1/4-1/2 tsp ghee with the meals for better gut health.

  • Daily addition of curd and buttermilk along with meals is very important for probiotics and better gut health. So, do not miss on them.

  • Try avoiding sugar and use alternatives like jaggery, brown sugar or pure honey.

  • It would be better to replace white rice with brown rice, quinoa or millets, if it is suiting you. Use whole wheat Ô¨Çour and avoid maida.

  • Avoid any processed, sugary or junk foods especially brought from outside.
